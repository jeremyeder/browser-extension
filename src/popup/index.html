Welcome to ACP

It looks like this is your first time connecting to the Agent Control Plane.

The first step is to build your personal Enterprise Assistant — an AI that goes with you everywhere. It connects to your systems of record — Google Workspace, Jira, GitHub, GitLab — and develops long-term memory about how you work.

Over time, your EA becomes a second brain at work: helping you find information, automate recurring tasks, and stay on top of what matters — through the CLI, browser extension, web UI, or mobile app.

Security Policies

Your Enterprise Assistant enforces these organizational security policies. These cannot be disabled.

  • Credentials, API keys, and tokens are protected by platform policy
  • Personally identifiable information is redacted in outputs
  • Dependencies are checked for known vulnerabilities
  • Infrastructure details are abstracted in responses
